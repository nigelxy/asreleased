Service line issues are sudden and can cost thousands of dollars to repair. Get the protection you need from potential water or sewer line expenses.

Repairing an outside water line or sewer line can cost homeowners thousands of dollars. It can be a sudden expense, and many homeowners don't have the cash on hand to cover the needed repairs. Typical homeowners' insurance policies do not cover the cost — and municipalities and water companies aren't responsible for service line repairs.

Water and sewer line issues can result in:

• Sewage backing up into your home and toilets clogging and overflowing
• Pooling water in the yard or in the home
• Low water pressure or unusually high water bills
• Pipes from washing machines, showers or sinks draining slowly or not at all
• Pipes to and from sinks leaking or clogging
• Inability to use shower, toilet and sink
